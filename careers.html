How We Work

The principles that shape every product decision, code review, and Monday morning.

01

Trust is the product

We build for industries — healthcare and education — where a broken promise has real consequences. Security, privacy, and correctness aren't features we add later. They're the table stakes.

02

Clarity beats cleverness

Plain language. Boring code. Documented decisions. The clever move is the one your future colleague can understand at 2am during an incident.

03

Ship, then learn

We'd rather ship a small thing this week and learn from it than discuss the perfect version for a quarter. Real users beat hypothetical ones.

04

Disagree, then commit

Strong opinions, loosely held. Push back, argue the case, change minds — including your own. Once the decision is made, row in the same direction.

05

Care about people

Users, colleagues, customers, partners. Treat everyone with respect and assume good intent. We don't do brilliant jerks.

06

Default to learning

The space we work in changes monthly. Cryptography evolves, standards mature, regulations shift. Curiosity isn't a bonus — it's the job.

Our Hiring Process

Transparent, structured, and respectful of your time. Four steps, roughly two weeks end-to-end.

01

Application Review

A real person reads what you sent. We don't use AI screening. You'll hear back within 5 business days either way — yes, no, or a thoughtful pass.

02

Intro Conversation 30 min

A two-way chat about what you've done, what you want, and what we're building. This is mostly about fit and motivation — no whiteboards.

03

Practical Exercise ~3 hours

A take-home that mirrors real work. We respect your time — short, paid where appropriate, and reviewed by the people you'd actually work with.

04

Final Conversations 2 × 45 min

Deeper technical discussion plus a conversation with a founder. You meet the team. We make a decision quickly — within a week.
